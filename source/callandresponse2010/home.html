In music, a call and response is a succession of two distinct phrases played by different musicians in which the second phrase is a commentary on or response to the first.

Named for this term, Call + Response is a collaboration between writers and visual artists: the writer provides the call, the artist provides the response. The result is paired works that resonate with each other and demonstrate the interplay of artistic media.
